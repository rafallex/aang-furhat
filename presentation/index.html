The piece I'm proudest of

He chooses when to transform

No keyword, no button. Every turn, the model weighs the moment — and when calm returns, it lets go.

The clever bit

Every reply carries a hidden cue — stay, or surge. I read it and drive the change. It can still be cued by a phrase or a key — but it doesn't need to be.

For the record

A text model — Groq · Llama 3.3 70B — reading the conversation, not the camera. The camera only helps Aang turn toward you.
